## Example 1

Input:
Develop a channel plan for a D2C skincare brand launching in the UAE, targeting women 25-40, with AED 200K budget over 3 months.

Output:
Complete channel plan with Instagram and TikTok as primary, Google Search and YouTube as secondary, influencer partnerships as support. Budget split: 40% paid social, 25% search, 20% video, 15% influencer. Integration flow from awareness through conversion with retargeting sequences.

## Example 2

Input:
Create a B2B channel strategy for a SaaS company targeting enterprise IT decision-makers in GCC.

Output:
LinkedIn-first strategy with programmatic display retargeting, Google Search for intent capture, and industry event sponsorship. Budget weighted 45% LinkedIn, 25% search, 20% programmatic, 10% events.
