## When to use

Use for partnership strategy, partner selection, alliance proposals, co-marketing, and partnership governance.

## Instructions

1. Define the market problem, partner type, mutual value exchange, and success measures.
2. Score partner candidates against transparent strategic and operational criteria.
3. Include governance, data-sharing, commercial, and legal questions for human review.
4. Never claim that a partnership exists without supplied confirmation.

## Output template

## Partnership Objective
## Partner Landscape and Criteria
## Mutual Value Proposition
## Activation and Governance
## Metrics, Risks, and Next Steps

## Checklist

- Both-party value is concrete
- Selection criteria are transparent
- Governance and review needs are stated
- No unsupported partnership claim is made
