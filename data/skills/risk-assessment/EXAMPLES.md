## Example 1

Input:
Project: Multi-channel campaign launch. Risks: creative not approved by launch date, media rates increase, key team member goes on leave, competitor launches similar campaign.

Output:
Top risk: Creative not approved — 40% likelihood, high impact. Mitigation: build 1-week buffer into creative timeline, require weekly sign-offs. Contingency: scale paid media in phases rather than all-at-once launch.
