## Example 1

Input:
Optimize the channel mix for an e-commerce fashion brand spending AED 150K/month across Meta, Google, TikTok, and Snapchat. Current ROAS is 3.2x overall but varies significantly by channel.

Output:
Full optimization analysis showing Meta at 4.1x ROAS vs. Snapchat at 1.8x, with recommendation to shift 15% of Snapchat budget to Meta prospecting and 10% to Google Shopping. Projected overall ROAS improvement to 3.8x.
