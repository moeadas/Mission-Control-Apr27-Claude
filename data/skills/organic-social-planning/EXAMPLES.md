## Example 1

Input:
Plan organic social strategy for a UAE-based wellness brand targeting women 25-40 on Instagram and TikTok.

Output:
5-pillar strategy: wellness tips (30%), product education (25%), user stories (20%), behind-the-scenes (15%), trending content (10%). Instagram: 4 posts + 7 stories/week. TikTok: 3 videos/week. Engagement rate targets: IG 4%, TT 6%.
