## 1. Objective Definition
Action: Define what decisions the test will inform
Verify: Objectives are clear and actionable

## 2. Concept Selection
Action: Select 2-4 concepts to test
Verify: Concepts are distinct enough to compare

## 3. Instrument Design
Action: Design survey or discussion guide
Verify: Instrument captures decision-relevant data

## 4. Execution
Action: Conduct testing with target audience
Verify: Participants are representative

## 5. Analysis
Action: Score concepts and synthesize feedback
Verify: Analysis is objective and evidenced

## 6. Recommendation
Action: Recommend which concept(s) to proceed with
Verify: Recommendation is clear and evidenced
