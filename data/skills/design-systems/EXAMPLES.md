## Example 1

Input:
Brand: Fast-growing D2C skincare brand. Scope: Social ad templates + display ads. Existing: logo, primary colors, basic typography. Platforms: Meta, Google, TikTok.

Output:
Design system: 4 color palettes (hero, benefit-led, ingredient, lifestyle), 3 typography scales, 6 button variants, 8 ad template formats (feed, stories, display, etc.). Each template includes safe zone guides, logo placement rules, and headline length guidelines. Component library in Figma with usage documentation.
