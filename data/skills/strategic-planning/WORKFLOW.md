## 1. Situation diagnosis
Action: Analyze brand health data, market position, and competitive landscape
Verify: Diagnosis validated with available data

## 2. Define strategic challenge
Action: Articulate the core strategic problem the brand must solve
Verify: Challenge statement approved by brand lead

## 3. Develop positioning
Action: Build a clear, ownable, differentiated brand positioning
Verify: Positioning tested against competitive set

## 4. Define audience profiles
Action: Create insight-driven target audience profiles with unmet needs
Verify: Audiences grounded in real insights

## 5. Build brand framework
Action: Define purpose, values, personality, and territory
Verify: Framework resonates with creative and strategic teams

## 6. Translate to strategic imperatives
Action: Convert positioning into actionable strategic priorities
Verify: Imperatives are specific and measurable

## 7. Define success metrics
Action: Tie metrics to business outcomes and brand goals
Verify: Metrics approved by client
