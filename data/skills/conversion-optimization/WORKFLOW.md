## 1. Baseline Audit
Action: Establish current conversion rate and drop-off points
Verify: Baseline is accurate and documented

## 2. Friction Identification
Action: Identify where and why users abandon
Verify: Friction points are evidenced

## 3. Hypothesis Generation
Action: Generate testable hypotheses
Verify: Hypotheses are specific and falsifiable

## 4. Prioritization
Action: Rank hypotheses by impact and effort
Verify: Highest priority items are clear

## 5. Testing
Action: Design and run A/B tests
Verify: Tests are statistically valid

## 6. Iteration
Action: Apply winning changes and continue testing
Verify: Conversion rate is improving
