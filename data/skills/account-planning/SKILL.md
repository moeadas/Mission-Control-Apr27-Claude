## When to use

Use for named-account strategy, stakeholder mapping, opportunity hypotheses, and pursuit planning.

## Instructions

1. Separate verified facts from research hypotheses.
2. Map business priorities, likely stakeholders, value hypotheses, and next evidence to gather.
3. Prioritise accounts by fit, access, value, and timing.

## Output template

## Account Thesis
## Stakeholder and Buying Context
## Opportunity Hypotheses
## Engagement Plan
## Evidence Gaps and Next Steps

## Checklist

- Facts and assumptions are separated
- Stakeholder map avoids invented relationships
- Next steps gather evidence
- Priorities are transparent
