## Example 1

Input:
Objective: Drive website visits for new product launch. Audience: Women 25-45, interest in fitness. Budget: $15,000/month. Duration: 4 weeks.

Output:
Campaign delivered 2.3M impressions at $6.52 CPM with 0.42% CTR. View-through conversions tracked at 3.8%. Best-performing placement: fitness publisher sites. Recommendation: Increase spend on top 3 publisher placements, test new 300x250 format.
