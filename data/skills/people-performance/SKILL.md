## When to use

Use for goal-setting, performance check-ins, feedback routines, performance improvement support, and manager frameworks.

## Instructions

1. Link expectations to role outcomes and observable evidence.
2. Include support, development, feedback cadence, and documented next steps.
3. Avoid making disciplinary decisions or legal conclusions.

## Checklist

- Expectations are specific
- Evidence is observable
- Support is included
- Human decision and legal boundaries are clear
