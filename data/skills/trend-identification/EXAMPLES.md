## Example 1

Input:
Metrics: CPC, CTR, ROAS. Time period: Daily over last 2 weeks. Benchmarks: Last 30-day average. Alert threshold: >15% change.

Output:
Alert triggered: CPC up 23% since Tuesday with no corresponding increase in conversions. ROAS down from 4.2x to 2.8x. Likely cause: competitor entered auction aggressively. Action: Increase bids gradually, monitor quality score, wait 48 hours before major changes.
