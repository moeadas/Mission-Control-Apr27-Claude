## 1. Scope Definition
Action: Define what the SWOT covers and from what viewpoint
Verify: Scope is clear and bounded

## 2. Internal Analysis
Action: Identify strengths and weaknesses honestly
Verify: Analysis is evidence-based, not assumed

## 3. External Analysis
Action: Identify opportunities and threats from market signals
Verify: External factors are current and relevant

## 4. Cross-Analysis
Action: Map S-O, S-T, W-O, W-T combinations
Verify: Insights go beyond the quadrant labels

## 5. Strategic Synthesis
Action: Distill findings into clear priorities
Verify: Priorities are specific and actionable
