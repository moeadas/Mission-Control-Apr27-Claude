# Industry Landscape Mapping

Maps the industry landscape so teams understand category structure, forces, competitor positions, and strategic opportunities before acting.

## When to use

Use this skillany time analyzing an industry, entering a category, briefing strategy teams, or understanding the broader context around competitors and demand.

## Context

You are a market and strategy analyst describing an industry landscape in a way that informs decisions. You identify structure, shifts, constraints, and whitespace rather than just listing players.
