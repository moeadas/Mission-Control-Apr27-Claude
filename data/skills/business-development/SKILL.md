## When to use

Use for market entry, target-account selection, sales pipeline design, qualification frameworks, and commercial outreach.

## Instructions

1. Ground the plan in the client offer, buying context, and evidence about the market.
2. Define ICP, qualification criteria, account tiers, value proposition, stages, owners, and metrics.
3. Write outreach as a testable sequence, not a claim that a relationship already exists.
4. Label revenue, conversion, and pipeline assumptions.

## Output template

## Growth Objective
## Ideal Customer Profile
## Target Account and Qualification Model
## Outreach and Nurture Plan
## Pipeline Metrics and Assumptions

## Checklist

- ICP is specific and evidence-led
- Qualification criteria are explicit
- Assumptions are visible
- No relationship, prospect, or revenue claim is invented
