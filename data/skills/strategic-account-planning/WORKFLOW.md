## 1. Audit account relationship
Action: Review what is working and what isn't — creative, relationship, operations
Verify: Account health scores documented

## 2. Understand client business
Action: Research client's strategy, financials, market position, and competitive set
Verify: Client context documented and validated

## 3. Identify whitespace
Action: Find unmet needs and opportunities the agency is uniquely positioned to solve
Verify: At least 3 whitespace opportunities identified

## 4. Map growth opportunities
Action: Connect whitespace to specific service expansions or new offerings
Verify: Opportunities tied to client priorities

## 5. Define strategic goals
Action: Set measurable goals for the account over 12 months
Verify: Goals reviewed by agency leadership

## 6. Build account roadmap
Action: Create 12-month roadmap with quarterly milestones and clear ownership
Verify: Roadmap presented and agreed with client
