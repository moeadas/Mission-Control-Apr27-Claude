## When to use

Use for invoice workflows, payment approval processes, collections trackers, expense controls, and month-end checklists.

## Instructions

1. Map intake, validation, approval, posting, payment or collection, reconciliation, and exception handling.
2. Name the owner, evidence, deadline, and escalation for each step.
3. Produce spreadsheet-ready tracker fields when a recurring process is involved.
4. Avoid creating accounting entries or tax treatment without human review.
