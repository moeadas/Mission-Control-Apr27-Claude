## Example 1

Input:
Research objective: Measure brand awareness and ad recall for new campaign. Target audience: US adults 25-54 who shop online weekly. Survey type: Online panel. Sample size: 500.

Output:
Designed 12-question survey with 3 brand awareness measures (aided, unaided, top-of-mind), 4 ad recall questions, and 5 demographic questions. Estimated completion time: 6 minutes. Margin of error: ±4.4% at 95% confidence.
