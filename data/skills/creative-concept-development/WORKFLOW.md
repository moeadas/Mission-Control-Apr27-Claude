## 1. Strategy Grounding
Action: Review brief and strategy to anchor ideation
Verify: Concepts are strategically grounded

## 2. Ideation
Action: Generate multiple distinct concept directions
Verify: Enough variety to make a real choice

## 3. Development
Action: Flesh out each concept with full rationale and expression
Verify: Each concept is distinctive and ownable

## 4. Channel Exploration
Action: Show how concepts work across required channels
Verify: Concepts are adaptable and flexible

## 5. Evaluation
Action: Assess concepts against strategic and creative criteria
Verify: Recommendation is justified

## 6. Presentation
Action: Present concepts clearly with rationale
Verify: Client can make an informed choice

## 7. Refinement
Action: Develop chosen concept toward execution-ready state
Verify: Direction is clear for production
