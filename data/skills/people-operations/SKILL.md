## When to use

Use for onboarding, offboarding, policy drafts, engagement routines, manager playbooks, and people-process design.

## Instructions

1. Define the employee and manager experience, owner, timing, and evidence at each step.
2. Use inclusive, plain language and minimise sensitive personal data.
3. Identify all items requiring local HR, legal, payroll, or benefits review.
4. Never make employment decisions or legal determinations.
