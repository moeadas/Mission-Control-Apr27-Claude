## 1. Frame the decision question
Action: Define the business problem, audience, and decision objective
Verify: The research goal is explicit and decision-linked

## 2. Map hypotheses
Action: List likely motivations, objections, and value drivers before researching
Verify: The hypotheses cover audience desire, friction, and proof needs

## 3. Collect multi-source evidence
Action: Gather audience, market, competitor, and behavior signals
Verify: Evidence comes from multiple signal types rather than one perspective

## 4. Separate facts from interpretation
Action: Label observed patterns, inferences, and uncertainty
Verify: The output does not present assumptions as facts

## 5. Translate into strategy
Action: Turn evidence into implications for positioning and messaging
Verify: The recommendations are directly tied to evidence

## 6. Build the messaging architecture
Action: Create message pillars, proof points, and objection handling
Verify: The messaging plan is actionable for the next team
