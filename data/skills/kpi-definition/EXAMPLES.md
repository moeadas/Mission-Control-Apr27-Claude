## Example 1

Input:
Define KPIs for an e-commerce brand spending AED 200K/month across Meta, Google, and TikTok.

Output:
Primary KPI: ROAS (target 4x). Secondary: CPA (<AED 50), CTR (>1.5%), ACOS (<25%). Channel-specific: Meta CPA <AED 45, Google ROAS 5x, TikTok CPV <AED 0.15. Weekly optimization review, monthly client report.
