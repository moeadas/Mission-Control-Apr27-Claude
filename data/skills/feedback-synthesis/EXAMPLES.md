## Example 1

Input:
Sources: CMO ('make it more bold'), VP Brand ('too aggressive, pull back color'), Designer ('they want more contrast'). Content: 15 pieces of feedback on homepage redesign. Deliverable: Homepage hero banner.

Output:
Synthesis: 3/4 sources want bolder, 1 wants subtler. True consensus: 'needs to stand out more' (all sources). Conflict: boldness vs. aggression — resolved by focusing on contrast and typography rather than brighter colors. Action item: Increase headline contrast, test 2 color variations with CMO approval before finalizing.
