## Example 1

Input:
Occasion: New business pitch to prospective client CMO. Audience: CMO and VP Marketing. Message: We're the right partner to rebuild their brand. Duration: 20 minutes + Q&A.

Output:
Opening hook: 'Three years ago their brand was everywhere. Today nobody remembers it. Here's why — and how we fix it.' Structure: Problem diagnosis (5 min), Our approach (8 min), Two case studies (5 min), Why us (2 min). Q&A prep: hardest question — 'Why should we trust you over our current agency?' Response: specific proof points from case study.
