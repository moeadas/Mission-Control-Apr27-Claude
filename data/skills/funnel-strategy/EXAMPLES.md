## Example 1

Input:
Design a full-funnel strategy for a new D2C fitness brand launching in UAE targeting health-conscious adults 25-45.

Output:
5-stage funnel: YouTube/TikTok for awareness (35%), Instagram for consideration (25%), Google Search/Shopping for intent (20%), Meta conversion campaigns (15%), email/loyalty for retention (5%). Retargeting: video viewers > Instagram engagement > site retargeting > post-purchase upsell.
