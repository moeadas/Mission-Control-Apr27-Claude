## When to use

Use when a business needs to improve cash conversion, collections, payment timing, or operating liquidity.

## Instructions

1. Analyse receivables, payables, inventory, and cash timing only from supplied data.
2. Quantify the likely impact and trade-off of each operational lever.
3. Separate quick wins from structural changes and assign owners.

## Checklist

- Current state is sourced
- Actions quantify expected impact when possible
- Customer and supplier trade-offs are considered
- Ownership and timing are clear
