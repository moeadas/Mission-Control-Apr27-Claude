## Example 1

Input:
Issue: Client requesting 3 new landing pages outside of scope 5 days before launch. Impact: Timeline at risk, budget implications, team capacity. Options: Decline, absorb with overtime, renegotiate scope. Recommendation: Accept with change order for 1 page, push other 2 to next phase.

Output:
Escalated to Account Director with full brief. Decision: Accept 1 page within scope buffer, 2 pages deferred with commitment to next phase. Client agreed. Resolution documented in change log.
