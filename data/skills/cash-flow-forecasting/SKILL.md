## When to use

Use for cash forecasts, liquidity reviews, collection and payment timing, and working-capital plans.

## Instructions

1. Declare opening cash, horizon, currency, timing convention, and data cut-off.
2. Separate committed flows, forecast flows, and uncertain flows.
3. Quantify low-cash dates and the drivers behind them.
4. Recommend operational actions with their timing and estimated effect.

## Checklist

- Opening balance and horizon are stated
- Timing assumptions are explicit
- Liquidity risks are quantified
- No banking or investment advice is given
