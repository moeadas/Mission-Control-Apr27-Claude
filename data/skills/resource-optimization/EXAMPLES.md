## Example 1

Input:
Resources: $50K monthly media budget, 3-person media team. Priorities: 2 active clients, 1 new business prospect. Current: budget split 70/30 between clients. Goal: better ROI across both accounts.

Output:
Found $8K/month was going to underperforming placements in Client B account. Rebalanced: shifted $8K to top-performing placements in Client A and added $5K test for new CTV inventory. Result: overall ROAS improved from 3.2x to 4.1x.
