## Example 1

Input:
Current state: Creative approval takes 5 days average. Goals: reduce to 2 days. Pain points: no clear escalation path, stakeholders travel without setting backups. Resources: Asana for project management.

Output:
Implemented: (1) clear 48-hour SLA for feedback, (2) required backup approver field in every project brief, (3) escalation trigger at 24 hours with automatic reminder. Result: average approval time reduced to 2.3 days within 6 weeks.
