## 1. Focal Point Check
Action: Identify if there is a clear focal point
Verify: Viewer knows where to look first

## 2. Hierarchy Assessment
Action: Check if visual priority is clear
Verify: Size, color, position establish priority

## 3. Balance Evaluation
Action: Assess layout balance
Verify: Layout feels stable and intentional

## 4. Flow Check
Action: Verify eye flow through the design
Verify: Flow is natural and guided

## 5. White Space Audit
Action: Review use of white space
Verify: White space is used effectively
