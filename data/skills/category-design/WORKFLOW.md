## 1. Opportunity Identification
Action: Find underserved or poorly defined space
Verify: Opportunity is real and sized

## 2. Category Definition
Action: Name and define the new category
Verify: Category is clear and ownable

## 3. Brand-Category Bond
Action: Build the brand as synonymous with the category
Verify: Brand is positioned as category leader

## 4. Market Education
Action: Create demand for the category itself
Verify: Market understands the category value

## 5. Defense Planning
Action: Build moats against competitors
Verify: Category is defensible
