## Example 1

Input:
Report type: Consumer research. Audience: Client brand team and agency. Findings: 68% of target segment prefers 'natural' positioning, competitor X is losing share among Gen Z, price sensitivity is decreasing. Objective: Inform brand repositioning strategy.

Output:
12-page report with 4 sections: market context, consumer insights, competitive dynamics, strategic recommendations. Key recommendation: shift positioning from 'premium quality' to 'natural ingredients' to capture growing segment. Supported by 6 charts and 2 consumer persona profiles.
