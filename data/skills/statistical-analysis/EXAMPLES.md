## Example 1

Input:
Data: CTRs for 3 ad creatives (A: 3.2%, B: 4.1%, C: 3.8%). Analysis type: One-way ANOVA. Variables: Creative variant, Hypothesis: At least one creative differs significantly.

Output:
F(2, 297) = 5.43, p = 0.005. Significant difference found. Post-hoc Tukey test: Creative B significantly outperforms A (p=0.003) and C (p=0.041). Effect size η² = 0.035 (small-medium). Recommend scaling Creative B.
