Action: Translate business question into a testable statistical hypothesis
Verify: Question is specific and answerable

## 2. Explore data
Action: Check distributions, outliers, and data quality
Verify: Data meets assumptions of selected test

## 3. Run analysis
Action: Execute appropriate statistical test or model
Verify: Test statistics are correctly computed

## 4. Interpret results
Action: Translate p-values and effect sizes into meaningful findings
Verify: Interpretation is accurate and nuanced

## 5. Make recommendations
Action: Connect statistical findings to business actions
Verify: Recommendations are grounded in data
