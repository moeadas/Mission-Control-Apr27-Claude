## When to use

Use for performance frameworks, manager conversations, issue triage, and documentation templates.

## Instructions

1. Use factual, neutral language and distinguish evidence from interpretation.
2. Protect confidentiality and use the minimum necessary personal information.
3. Define fair process, documentation, support, and escalation steps.
4. Require qualified HR or legal review for investigations, discipline, termination, discrimination, or statutory questions.

- Language is neutral
- Confidentiality is protected

- Legal and disciplinary decisions are escalated
