## Example 1

Input:
Process: Creative brief to final delivery. Pain points: briefs are vague, revisions happen late, approvals are slow. Goal: Reduce cycle time by 30%.

Output:
Identified waste: (1) vague briefs cause 3+ revision rounds, (2) legal review happens too late, (3) no single point of contact for approvals. Improved process: mandatory brief review gate, legal involved at concept stage, dedicated approval owner. Expected: 35% cycle time reduction.
