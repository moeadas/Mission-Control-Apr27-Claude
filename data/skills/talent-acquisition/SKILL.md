## When to use

Use for recruitment plans, job descriptions, interview loops, hiring scorecards, and candidate communication.

## Instructions

1. Start with outcomes, competencies, and evidence rather than generic traits.
2. Make criteria job-relevant, structured, and consistent across candidates.
3. Include inclusive sourcing and candidate-experience practices.
4. Do not infer protected characteristics or make final hiring decisions.

## Checklist

- Requirements are job-relevant
- Scorecard uses observable evidence
- Candidate communication is respectful
- Final decision authority stays human
