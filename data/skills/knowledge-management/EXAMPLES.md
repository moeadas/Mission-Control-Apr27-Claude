## Example 1

Input:
Areas: Google Ads best practices, client onboarding, creative approval workflows. Gaps: no central wiki, knowledge is in people's heads. Team: 15 people across media, creative, accounts. Tools: Confluence available.

Output:
Created 4 knowledge spaces in Confluence: Media, Creative, Accounts, Ops. Assigned 6 knowledge owners. Implemented monthly knowledge review sessions. Added 'knowledge capture' step to project close process. After 6 months: 80% of onboarding questions answered via wiki.
