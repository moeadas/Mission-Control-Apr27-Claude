## Example 1

Input:
Current scope: 3 landing pages. Change request: Client wants 3 additional pages and a different layout template. Contract: standard agency agreement with change order clause.

Output:
Assessed impact: 3 additional pages + new template = 16 additional hours at standard rate = $2,400. Presented options: (1) Decline — deliver original 3 pages on schedule, (2) Change order — add 3 pages and template for $2,400, 5 additional days. Client chose option 2. Change order signed.
