## Example 1

Input:
Purpose: Q1 performance review. Audience: Client CMO and VP of Marketing. Key message: Campaign outperformed benchmarks by 23% but we recommend shifting budget to video. Content: 3 months of performance data.

Output:
12-slide deck: 1 title, 1 executive summary, 4 slides on channel performance, 3 slides on budget analysis, 1 competitive context, 2 slides with video recommendation, 1 next steps. Strong visual: performance vs. benchmark chart on slide 3 as the 'hook.'
