## 1. Prepare Intelligence
Action: Research client situation, constraints, and history
Verify: You understand their likely position and pressures

## 2. Define Strategy
Action: Set targets, walk-aways, and concession ladder
Verify: Strategy is clear and team-aligned

## 3. Frame the Conversation
Action: Open with shared goals and value delivered
Verify: Conversation starts collaboratively

## 4. Negotiate
Action: Trade concessions, not give them away; listen actively
Verify: Making progress toward objectives without conceding too much

## 5. Document Outcome
Action: Capture agreed terms clearly and immediately
Verify: Both parties aligned on what was agreed

## 6. Post-Negotiation Review
Action: Assess outcome against objectives and document learnings
Verify: Future negotiations informed by this experience
