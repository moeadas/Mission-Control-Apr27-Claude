## 1. Diagnose gap
Action: Identify exactly where expectations diverge from reality
Verify: Gap is specific and quantified

## 2. Prepare context
Action: Gather data to support the conversation
Verify: Data is accurate and credible

## 3. Communicate proactively
Action: Have the conversation before the gap causes problems
Verify: Communication is timely and honest

## 4. Document agreement
Action: Confirm aligned expectations in writing
Verify: Agreement is clear and signed off
