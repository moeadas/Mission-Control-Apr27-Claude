## Example 1

Input:
Stakeholder: Client CMO. Expectation: Top 3 Google ranking for 10 keywords within 3 months. Reality: Current domain authority is low, top 3 requires 6-12 months minimum for competitive terms. Context: Q1 planning meeting.

Output:
Acknowledged the ambition, then provided realistic framing: 'For 2 of the 10 lower-competition keywords, we can realistically target top 10 in 3 months. For the 8 competitive terms, 6-12 months is more realistic. Here's what we recommend as an interim milestone...' Aligned agreement: top 10 for 6 keywords in 3 months, top 5 for 2 in 6 months.
