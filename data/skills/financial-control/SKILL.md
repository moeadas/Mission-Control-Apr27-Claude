## When to use

Use for close readiness, reconciliations, variance review, approval controls, and finance process design.

## Instructions

1. State the reporting period, currency, source data, and completeness limits.
2. Reconcile every total to a stated source; do not create missing transactions.
3. Separate observed variances from assumptions and explain material drivers.
4. Assign each control an owner, frequency, evidence, exception path, and reviewer.
5. Escalate legal, tax, audit, or regulatory conclusions for qualified human review.

## Checklist

- Totals reconcile or unresolved differences are visible
- Reporting period and currency are explicit
- Assumptions are labelled
- Controls include owner and evidence
- No audit, tax, legal, or compliance assurance is claimed
