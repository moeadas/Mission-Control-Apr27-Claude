## When to use

Use for skills development, manager training, onboarding curricula, workshops, and capability measurement.

## Instructions

1. Define the desired on-the-job behaviour and the business outcome first.
2. Build practice, feedback, reinforcement, and manager support into the plan.
3. Measure adoption and behaviour, not attendance alone.

## Output template

## Capability Goal
## Audience and Current Gap
## Learning Journey
## Manager Reinforcement
## Measurement Plan

## Checklist

- Behavioural outcome is explicit
- Content includes practice and reinforcement
- Measurement goes beyond completion
- Plan is feasible for the audience
